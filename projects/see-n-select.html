Project Intro

See & Select is VoiceComm's annual event, connecting VIP customers with vendors ahead of the year's iPhone launch - a key revenue opportunity for cell phone stores. The goal is to help customers plan their accessory purchases and provide a premium, early-access experience.

Online Conference

Staying Connected During Covid in 2021

See & Select debuted in 2021 as a fully virtual event due to the COVID-19 pandemic. To maintain a personalized touch, we designed a virtual meeting kit for participants, including bingo cards, door hangers, and other customized items. Additionally, a dedicated landing page with event details and the itinerary was created to streamline the experience.
